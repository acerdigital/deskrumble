why it works

Not another sci-fi shooter

Four things make Desk Rumble stick with people who'd normally scroll past a bullet-hell game.

1

Childhood nostalgia

Everyone had a desk battlefield in their head as a kid. This just makes it real.

2

Broad, gender-neutral appeal

No armor, no lore-heavy sci-fi. Stationery is universal, so the audience is too.

3

Objects built for combat

Pencils pierce, rulers swing, tape binds. The shapes were already made for it.

4

Real collecting behavior

People already hoard cute stationery in real life. This just gives it a scoreboard.
