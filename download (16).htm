About This Game

Explore a quiet Japanese neighborhood in this pixel art hidden object game focused on finding tiny cats.

Wander through narrow streets, small houses, and local shops while carefully examining each scene. You can enter buildings, look inside rooms, and interact with environmental elements to discover hidden locations and reveal cats that aren't immediately visible.

The city features small environmental details that give each area a unique feel, without distracting from the main experience.

Take your time, observe carefully, and see if you can find them all.
