About This Game

You are a traveler who has arrived in a strange, scripted world.

What begins as an ordinary journey soon leads into a growing mystery that threatens the foundations of Arcadia itself. Choose from a cast of starting protagonists, including a formidable Arcadian Knight burdened by a painful past, a traveling stage magician who thrives on unpredictable outcomes, and more to be announced. Uncover long-forgotten secrets and explore a world where every town, forest, and ruin hides stories waiting to be discovered.

Arcadia Unbound is a character-driven tactical JRPG that blends strategic combat, exploration, and narrative adventure into a single immersive experience.

FEATURES

■ An Evolution of the Classic Strategy RPG System

Lead your party through strategic battles that reward planning, positioning, and teamwork. Master each character's strengths and overcome increasingly dangerous threats as your adventure progresses.

Arcadia Unbound introduces the Augmented Continuous Tactical Movement (ACTM) system, evolving on the traditional movement method of classic games. Unlike traditional grid-based tactics games, Arcadia Unbound allows units to move freely across the battlefield within their movement range. Run across bridges, leap over obstacles, navigate elevated terrain, and preview attacks before committing to your actions. If the outcome is not preferable, reset your movement and try navigating to another area, or choose to retreat instead. Players have full freedom to test movement and abilities to ensure optimal use of their character's abilities with least hassle.

Discover more and more unique features of the combat as the game progresses:

  • Class Advancement: Every unit on the battlefield from party members to bosses to generic enemy units have their own class advancement tree. Every class is built with the intention of offering a different play-style, balancing varying strengths and weaknesses across all possible advancement options.

  • Terrain-Based Power-ups: Arcadia Unbound battlefield maps are unique and will contain complex layouts with varying heights and grounds littered with structures to climb over, traverse around, in order to get to your objectives. In addition to that, the game also utilizes an elemental system that allows characters to power up their abilities and skills depending on the terrain of the tile they are standing on. With the right conditions, players might discover their moves transform into a cinematic fire or wind-elemental version of the move with devastating effects. The same remains true for enemy units and bosses, so terrain utilization and map control are key to victory!

■ Discover AURA and Build Meaningful Bonds

No dialogue choices. No predefined responses to select from. Speak naturally using your own words.

Get to know your companions through dynamic conversations, personal stories, and shared adventures. Learn what motivates them, challenge their beliefs, and forge lasting relationships throughout your journey.

Use the AURA Framework to speak to characters during key moments with your own words and expressions.

  • Cinematic Conversation - During pivotal moments of the story, speak out to a character and support them or challenge their beliefs, their actions, and make your voice heard. Characters will react while you speak, may interrupt, and even push back. These may be your only chance to influence the shape of story and bonds, so make every word count! All spoken dialogue are fully voiced by professional voice actors.

  • Deep Dialogue - At towns and hubs, you may find your party members strolling about while winding down from the heat of battle. At towns and hubs, spend time with your companions through Deep Dialogue. Speak to them using your own words, ask questions, revisit past events, and uncover new sides of their personalities through personalized and immersive conversations unique to your journey.

■ Explore the World of Arcadia

Travel across diverse regions filled with unique cultures, mysteries, and hidden discoveries. From quiet villages to forgotten ruins, every location contributes to the larger story unfolding around you.

■ Choices That Shape Your Journey

The people you meet, the questions you ask, and the relationships you build all influence your experience. No two journeys through Arcadia need feel exactly the same.

■ A Story of Mystery and Adventure

Investigate the strange cracks appearing throughout the world and uncover the truth behind a force that threatens to reshape reality itself.

Independent Development

Arcadia Unbound is being independently developed by Apurva Saini, founder of Immersion Games Inc. Game design, writing, programming, gameplay systems, and development of the AURA Framework are led by a single developer in collaboration with talented artists, voice actors, composers, and production partners from around the world.

For a complete list of contributors and project credits, please visit the official website.

AI Generated Content Disclosure

The developers describe how their game uses AI Generated Content like this:

Arcadia Unbound does not use AI to generate player-facing dialogue, story content, quests, localization, music, sound effects, or voice acting.

Arcadia Unbound uses a locally running language model as part of the AURA Framework to interpret player speech during specific conversation sequences. The model analyzes player intent and conversational context, then selects from developer-authored dialogue, events, and narrative outcomes.

All player-facing content is creatively directed, authored, reviewed, refined, integrated, and curated by the development team, with professional voice actors performing character voices where applicable. No AI system generates dialogue, story events, audio, or player-facing content at runtime.

Like many modern development teams, Arcadia Unbound uses AI-assisted tools during development for artwork workflows, including research, early-stage exploration, and upscaling certain existing assets.

A full list of contributors and production credits is available on our website.
